# Scene image research

- One image per hook/point/stat beat — not every beat needs imagery.
- Prefer official logos, product UI, or clean stock photos with vertical crop headroom.
- Search queries must be concrete: `"Notion app logo png"` not `"productivity"`.
- Validate with Serper image search before finalizing queries.
- Downloaded assets land in `output/<run>/assets/images/` and sync to `remotion/public/runs/<run>/`.
